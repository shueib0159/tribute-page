Grace Hopper

Computer Science Pioneer

Rear Admiral Grace Hopper

Timeline

  • 1906: Born in New York City.
  • 1934: PhD in Mathematics from Yale.
  • 1943: Joined US Navy Reserve.
  • 1944: Worked on Harvard Mark I.
  • 1952: Invented the first compiler.
  • 1959: Helped develop COBOL.
  • 1991: Won National Medal of Technology.
